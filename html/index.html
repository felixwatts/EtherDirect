How does it work?

❶ Get an Ethereum account

If you don't have one yet don't worry, it's free.

We recommend Metamask or MyCrypto

❷ Make a payment

When you're ready, arrange a bank transfer to our account.

The amount must be between £5 and £20.

In the Reference/Message field put the address of your Ethereum account

❸ Receive your Ether

Once we receive your payment, we take a fixed £1 service charge and use the rest of the money to source Ether at a competitive price

We transfer the Ether directly to your Ethereum account. It should arrive within minutes
